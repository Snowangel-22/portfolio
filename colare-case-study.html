The Problem

Two layers of issues

Operational

  • Em-dash encoding corrupted on all 9 pages — rendered as ,
  • 12+ links pointing to href="#" — dead on click
  • Navigation chain had a loop: Proctoring → Dashboard → Proctoring
  • Email domain mismatch: colare.co vs colare.com
  • Search bar rendered but had no behavior

Product

  • 8 employer guides listed flat — no workflow context
  • Quick-access chips jumped to articles, bypassing the filter system
  • TOC sidebar had no active state — no indication of reading position
  • Employer and candidate sections used inconsistent heading patterns
  • No visual distinction between interactive and static figures
