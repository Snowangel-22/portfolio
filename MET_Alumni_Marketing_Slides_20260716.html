Step 1 of 5 · Foundation

Build one shared contact database

Importance
Contact data is scattered across organizers' personal circles and events are shared "word of mouth"
A shared database helps with outreach, events, and onboarding
Enables consistent, coordinated communication across all ~300 alumni
Better tracks feedback across events to improve them over time
Proposed Tasks
1
Collect alumni contacts: name, cohort year, email, LinkedIn URL, phone (?)
2
Merge into one shared Google Sheet, accessible to all leads
3
Use M.E.T. LinkedIn alumni group and cohort Slack lists to recover missing contacts
4
One person maintains and updates the list going forward
