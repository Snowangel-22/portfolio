About Me


Hi I'm Angela!

I studied EECS and Business Administration at UC Berkeley through the MET program. Today, I work as a Product Manager at Applied Materials, where I spend a lot of time with customers around the world solving real, high-stakes problems. That can mean getting hands-on with physical tools one moment and thinking through software or hardware solutions the next.

If I had to name a superpower, it’s making things happen. I’m outcome-driven and comfortable navigating ambiguity—give me a goal, and by the next time we talk, it’ll be moving forward (or already done).

Why product?

The thing I love most about product is that you're never stuck in one lane. One hour I'm talking architecture with engineers, the next I'm looking at user feedback, then I'm thinking through go-to-market strategy. I enjoy being at the intersection of it all—connecting the “why” behind decisions with how a company actually executes. I work best when I’m juggling multiple threads, and the variety keeps me energized and focused.

Outside of work I enjoy redesigning by apartment, watching Broadway musicals, and going to Lagree.
