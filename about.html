About Me


Hi I'm Angela!

I studied EECS and Business Administration at UC Berkeley through the MET program. As a PM at Applied Materials, I travel to customers around the world to solve their biggest problems. Everything from understanding physical tools to developing software and hardware solutions.

I'm also a tinkerer at heart. I'm always prototyping products I wish existed and scripting little tools to make my workflow faster.

Why product?

The thing I love most about product is that you're never stuck in one lane. One hour I'm talking architecture with engineers, the next I'm looking at user feedback, then I'm thinking through go-to-market strategy. I get to be a polyglot with a bird's-eye view—understanding why decisions matter and how all the pieces of the business fit together. I'm energized by complex systems and competing constraints. Plus, I work better when I'm juggling multiple things at once—the variety keeps me engaged, and constantly switching contexts forces me to be great at prioritization.

Outside of work I enjoy browsing IKEA, watching broadway musicals, and redesigning my apartment.
