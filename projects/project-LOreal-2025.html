Project Overview

Pitch an idea that follow the 2025 competition's theme: Men. Beauty & Care. Game on.

Our idea is LuminAl, a personalized skincare routine builder powered by AI. Whenever the user tries a new product, it links product information (such as ingredients) with user recorded feedback. It will find commonalities and personalize the routine to help the user work towards their skincare goal.

Most importantly, we want skincare to be fun. LuminAl makes trying skincare social and competitive in order to be the best version of yourself.

Process

There are three main points the team considered:

  • Beauty related actions for men today
  • Areas of market growth (user trends) and personas
  • Build for existing habits vs creating new behaviors

It was also important to decide how the product will integrate into the existing L'Oreal brands. Out of the market funnel - awareness, consideration, conversion, and loyalty - we wanted to focus on consideration and conversion.

User Segment

We decided to build for young, curious Gen Z and Millennial men who are interested in skincare but don't know where to start. This group wants to take care of their skin but feels overwhelmed by the sheer number of products, conflicting advice, and terminology they don't understand. They're willing to invest time and money into looking better, but they need a clear starting point and guidance they can trust.

Painpoints

  • Information overload
  • Lack of personalized guidance
  • Skincare feels intimidating

We focused on addressing the lack of personalized guidance because skincare is fundamentally unique and personal. When someone learns an ingredient clears their breakouts or irritates their skin, that becomes valuable knowledge. Giving the right information builds trust and turns curious experimenters into loyal customers.
