Project Overview

Designed and implemented my own portfolio website to showcase my projects and skills.

Challenge

I needed a centralized place where I can showcase my projects and skills.

Process

The vision of this portfolio is to complement my resume and give a comprehensive overview of the type of projects I enjoy doing. A bonus would be if this leads to the next exciting opportunity. It needed to be simple, easy to read, and have room to add cool features later.

I decided to start with a template because I do not want design paralysis to be the first roadblock I encounter. I browsed through portfolios of my peers, and found that the ease of navigating through the page is much more important than the visual design.

The second consideration was whether the website needs a backend. To an average person browsing, I do not think they will spend more than one to five minutes glancing through every page, almost like skimming a resume. Most of my deliverables in projects and work are decks, briefs, and at most videos, so I didn't really see the need for server connection.

Finally, a non-negotiable feature of this portfolio was being mobile friendly. Please let me know if you find bugs, I will trade you mint chocolate.

Solution

Given my goals, I will be hosting this on github for now. I am planning to do the version 0 launch with the following features:

  • Project page for each project
  • Website header and footer
  • Working links to my resume and social

Result

The portfolio website is now live and can be accessed at https://snowangel-22.github.io/portfolio/.

Improvements

  • And maybe a blog?
