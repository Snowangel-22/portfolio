E-commerce Platform Redesign

Complete UX overhaul resulting in 40% increase in conversion rates

Back to Projects

Project Overview

Led a comprehensive redesign of a major e-commerce platform's user experience, focusing on improving conversion rates and user satisfaction. The project involved extensive user research, A/B testing, and iterative design improvements.

Challenge

The existing platform had a high bounce rate and low conversion rates. Users were struggling with the checkout process and product discovery. The design was outdated and not mobile-responsive.

Solution

Implemented a user-centered design approach with the following key improvements:

  • Streamlined checkout process with fewer steps
  • Improved product search and filtering capabilities
  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Enhanced product recommendation engine
  • Simplified navigation structure

Results

The redesign resulted in a 40% increase in conversion rates, 25% reduction in bounce rate, and 60% improvement in mobile user engagement. Customer satisfaction scores increased by 35%.

Technologies Used

Product Management UX Design A/B Testing User Research Figma Analytics

Timeline

Duration: 6 months

Team Size: 8 people

Role: Product Manager
